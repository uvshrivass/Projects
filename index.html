Customer Segment

Value Proposition

Things to consider:

b. Which customer needs are you satisfying?


Channels

Things to consider:

a. Through which channels that your customers want to be reached?
b. Which channels work best? How much do they cost?


Customer Relationships

Things to consider:

a. What relationship that the target customer expects you to establish?
b. How can you integrate that into your business in terms of cost and format?


Revenue Streams

Things to consider:

a. For what value are your customers willing to pay?
b. What and how do they recently pay? How would they prefer to pay?

Key Partners

a. Who are your key partners/suppliers?
b. What are the motivations for the partnerships?
